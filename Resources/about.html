MW2 Map Tactics is all about MW2 Maps and Tactics. Why is it better than other apps in App Store? This app has ALL the maps, including maps from Stimulus and Resurgence Map Packs!

The app contains about 160+ detailed, high quality map layouts for different game modes such as Team Deathmatch, Search & Destroy, Domination, Headquarters, Capture The Flag, Demolition, Sabotage...

Features

160+ high quality maps for all 26 maps (which includes the stimulus and resurgence map packs).

Maps can be zoomed in and out either by pinching or double-tapping.

Tips and tactics for every map whether you are a run 'n gun type, or a camper, or sniper or ninja.

Videos for every map from famous players of the Modern Warfare 2 game!
